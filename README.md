Customer Churn Analytics Dashboard
Project Overview

Developed an end-to-end Customer Churn Analytics Dashboard using SQL and Power BI to analyze customer behavior, identify churn patterns, evaluate revenue impact, and provide actionable business insights. The project analyzes a dataset of 10,000 customers and transforms raw data into meaningful business intelligence through interactive dashboards.

Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts revenue and business growth. The objective of this project was to:

Measure customer churn and retention rates.
Identify revenue loss caused by churned customers.
Analyze churn across regions, subscription plans, and product categories.
Segment customers based on spending behavior.
Highlight opportunities for customer retention and revenue growth.

Key Business Insights

📉 Churn Rate: 27.28%

🔄 Retention Rate: 72.72%

💰 Revenue Lost Due to Churn: ₹118.9M

💵 Average Revenue Lost per Churned Customer: ₹43.6K

👑 High-Value Customers generated the highest revenue contribution.

📦 Low-Value Customer Segment contributed the highest share of churn (54.77%).

🌎 West Region generated the highest overall revenue.

🏆 Basic Subscription generated the largest customer base and revenue contribution.
